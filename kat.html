Kat-B320

Callsign: Noble Two - Lieutenant Commander

Biography

Kat-B320 serves as Noble Team's brilliant second-in-command and chief tactician. Recruited into SPARTAN‑III Beta Company after losing her family to Covenant attacks, Kat quickly earned a reputation for lightning-fast analysis and daring infiltration tactics.

An earlier mission cost her left arm; she now wields an advanced cybernetic prosthetic integral to her custom MJOLNIR Mk. V suit, which also houses an upgraded data-link for on-the-fly network intrusion. Kat prefers speed and precision over brute force—often carrying a M6G sidearm and data-pad rather than heavy ordnance.

On Reach she masterminded the intel heist at SWORD Base and co-authored plans for Operation: UPPER CUT. During the civilian evacuation of New Alexandria (August 12, 2552), Kat was struck and killed instantly by a Needle-rifle round from a Covenant Zealot sniper—an abrupt loss that rattled the remaining squad and underscored the brutal efficiency of the Covenant occupation.
