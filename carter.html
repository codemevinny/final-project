Carter-A259

Callsign: Noble One - Commander

Biography

Carter-A259 is the calm and decisive Commander of Noble Team. Orphaned by Covenant aggression during the early Human-Covenant War, he was inducted into SPARTAN-III Beta Company and distinguished himself in covert operations before being hand-selected by Colonel Urban Holland to lead Noble Team on Reach.

Known for his tactical acumen and unwavering loyalty to his squad, Carter pilots MJOLNIR Mk.V armor distinguished by a custom-painted blue visor stripe and hardened command uplink module. He values thorough reconnaissance and surgical strikes over brute force, though he is more than capable in direct engagements.

During the Fall of Reach (July-August 2552), Carter oversaw critical missions ranging from investigating the Viery Territories communications blackout to escorting AI fragment Cortana toward the Pillar of Autumn.
