Jun-A266

Callsign: Noble Three - Warrant Officer

Biography

Jun-A266 is Noble Team's lone marksman and reconnaissance specialist—patient, pragmatic, and renowned for near-preternatural accuracy with the Sniper Rifle System 99.

A fellow orphan of Covenant aggression, Jun volunteered for SPARTAN-III Beta Company and quickly distinguished himself on Sabre-cat insertion missions where stealth and surgical precision were paramount. His custom MJOLNIR Mk. V armor carries a hooded camouflage shroud and enhanced optics suite, reflecting his role as forward scout.

During Reach's fall he provided overwatch at SWORD Base, spearheaded the civilian evacuation of New Alexandria, and served as Guardian sniper during Operation: UPPER CUT. Unlike the rest of Noble, Jun ultimately survived the campaign—tasked with escorting Dr. Catherine Halsey and critical ONI intel off-world. After the war he became chief instructor for the nascent Spartan‑IV program, ensuring his brothers' legacy endured.
