The Covenant

Religious-military hegemony (Ages of Doubt & Reclamation)

Overview

The Covenant is a theocratic alliance of alien species bound by a shared worship of the Forerunners and pursuit of the so-called Great Journey. By 2552—the events depicted in Halo: Reach—the alliance had waged a 27-year genocidal campaign against humanity, deeming them an affront to Forerunner divinity.

Governed by the triumvirate of Hierarchs (Prophets Truth, Mercy, and Regret) from their mobile capital High Charity, day-to-day military operations were delegated to a High Council of Sangheili (Elites) and San'Shyuum (Prophets). Fleet actions on Reach fell under the Supreme Command of Fleet Master Thel 'Vadamee (the future Arbiter).

Key Member Species & Roles

  • San'Shyuum (Prophets): Political & religious leadership.
  • Sangheili (Elites): Naval command, frontline combat, honor‑bound warrior caste.
  • Jiralhanae (Brutes): Shock troops, heavy infantry (limited Reach deployment, rising prominence).
  • Kig'Yar (Jackals) & Skirmishers: Scouts, snipers, naval boarding.
  • Unggoy (Grunts): Mass infantry, support roles.
  • Yanme'e (Drones): Aerial harassment, ship maintenance.
  • Lekgolo (Hunters): Bonded shield/assault pairs, heavy armor assets.

Campaign on Reach - Key Dates (2552)

  1. July 24: Covenant Zealot strike team establishes stealth relay in Viery Territory.
  2. July 26: Covenant corvette group makes atmosphere entry; first ground engagements near Visegrád.
  3. August 14: Supercarrier Long Night of Solace arrives; ultimately destroyed by UNSC Operation:UPPER CUT.
  4. August 15-30: Main invasion fleet drops; Covenant secures New Alexandria, Aszod, and Eposz zones.
  5. August 30: Pillar of Autumn departs; Covenant begin planet-wide glassing.
  6. September 23 (approx.): Reach officially declared lost; residual Covenant cleanup operations commence.

Strategic Objectives on Reach

  • Destroy UNSC Epsilon Eridani fleet anchor and shipyards.
  • Eliminate ONI research assets (notably CORTANA fragments and SWORD Base projects).
  • Capture navigation data leading to Earth and other colonies.
  • Prevent humanity from fielding next-gen Sabre-class suborbital fighters.

Legacy & Aftermath

The Covenant's victory at Reach was pyrrhic; although they glassed humanity's principal fortress-world, Noble Team's actions ensured the AI Cortana and the frigate Pillar of Autumn escaped to uncover Installation 04—setting in motion events that would fracture the Covenant three months later at Halo.
